Org Roam

An Effortless Personal Knowledge Management System

FEATURES

Private and Portable

All notes are stored in your local machine without any analytics whatsoever. Your data is yours, period. Notes are written in the plaintext org markup, and are always portable.

Backlinks

Backlinks are the core of Org Roam. For any note topic, you can see all the other notes that link to it.

Effortless

As all your notes are automatically centralized, you don't have to break flow to decide where a note should go.

Graph Visualization

Discover surprising and previously unseen connections in your notes with the build-in Graph Network Visualization.

Extensibility

Org Roam is built on top of Emacs and Org Mode, making their vast ecosystems easily integrable.
